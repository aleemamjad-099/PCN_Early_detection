The PCN Early Detection System screens patients using 18 biomarkers (13 clinical inputs and 5 domain-engineered biomarkers). Review the reference table below for clinical ranges and metrics.

Biomarker Clinical Type Reference Range Clinical Significance & Impact
Age Demographic 40 - 90 years Pancreatic Cancer risk doubles every decade after 50. Neurological decay rates scale heavily with age.
BMI Physical / Metabolic 16.0 - 55.0 kg/m² Obesity is a strong driver for diabetes, metabolic syndrome, and subsequent pancreatic stress.
HbA1c Glycaemic Marker 4.5 - 12.0% Indicator of chronic hyperglycaemia. Patients with recent-onset diabetes have up to 8x higher risk of Pancreatic Cancer.
LDL Cholesterol Lipid Profile 40 - 260 mg/dL Correlated with cardiovascular health, metabolic status, and neuro-lipid homeostasis.
AST & ALT Liver Enzymes 5 - 150 / 5 - 200 U/L Alanine Aminotransferase and Aspartate Aminotransferase measure hepatic stress and bile duct obstruction.
APOE-e4 Gene Genetic Flag Yes / No The single strongest genetic risk factor for Alzheimer's and cognitive impairment.
Memory Score Cognitive Metric 1 - 10 scale Clinical cognitive performance score. Drop below 6 suggests neurological deterioration.
Vitamin B12 Nutritional / Neuro 150 - 800 pg/mL Neuroprotective vitamin. Deficiencies lead to demyelination and mimic neurodegenerative disorders.

Ensemble Model Classifier

Our machine learning pipeline uses a Soft-Voting Ensemble Classifier that combines an XGBoost Classifier and a Logistic Regression model.

Model Performance Metrics:
• Ensemble ROC-AUC: 89.36%
• XGBoost ROC-AUC: 87.47%
• Accuracy (Test Set): 81.00%
• F1-Score (Weighted): 82.00%
• Risk Detection Cutoff: 40.00%

Pipeline Architecture

  • Preprocessing: Zero-mean, unit-variance Scaling using StandardScaler fitted on train split.
  • Balancing: Resampled minority class using Synthetic Over-sampling (SMOTE) to ensure high sensitivity to risk cases.
  • Hyperparameters: Optimized via 720 GridSearchCV fits. Includes 800 estimators, 0.03 learning rate, and L1/L2 regularisation.

The system utilizes a modern decoupled architecture. The frontend web panel communicates with dedicated microservices for Authentication and AI inference.
